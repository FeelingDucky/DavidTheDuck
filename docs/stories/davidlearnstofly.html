David Learns To Fly
Once upon a time, there was a duck named David who wanted to fly. He asked his friends how he could learn to fly. They suggested going to flight school. David the Duck enrolled in flight school the very next day, and started his journey to become a pilot. He worked hard. He learned how to read the sky charts, how to signal and sign, how to fly an airplane, and even how to do sharp turns in the air!

5 years later, he had a big test before he could graduate. David the Duck got almost EVERYTHING wrong! This made him very angry. This made him very upset. He had worked so hard, but he had still not succeeded.

David the Duck was SO angry that he started moving his arms up and down, and up and down, and up and down in frustration.

Before you know it, he was levitating off the ground. Soon he was flying on his own with all his flapping. He was so happy he flew all the way across the world to China!

The end.



The moral of the story is:
Don’t always listen to other people and do your own research.
