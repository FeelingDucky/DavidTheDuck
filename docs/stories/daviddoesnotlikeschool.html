David Does NOT Like School


en français


Once upon a time, there was a duck named David who did not like school. One day he had an idea: “What if I get a robot that looks like me and talks like me?” David said. “Then HE can go to school and I won’t have to!”

David ordered a robot on the internet, and it arrived quickly. David was super happy. “I will never have to go to school again!”

The very next day, Robot David went to school instead. No one knew that it was a robot. The real David was super happy at home, on the couch, watching TV.

Robot David did TOO well at school. He started winning lots of medals and prizes. Everyone liked him more. The real David started to feel jealous.

“Why can’t I win medals and prizes?” said the real David.
Robot David said “Because you do not go to school, and you do not work hard”.

David decided the robot had said too much. He took out the robot’s battery, put him back in the box, and shipped him back to China.

The next day, the real David started going back to school again. He had to work hard to win medals and prizes. He was super happy.

At the end of the year, David wanted to thank his teacher. He gave his teacher a box of chocolate balls as a gift. The teacher ate one, and said “why are these chocolate balls filled with toothpaste?”

“DAVAAAAAD!”



The moral of the story:
Robots might be good at school, you can order anything on the internet, and you should ALWAYS check your chocolate for toothpaste before you eat it.
